When do you need Therapy / Counselling?

If you feeling upset for most part of the day for 2 weeks or more.

If you are not able to stop yourself from constantly worried over something.

If you are not able to sleep well.

Don't feel like interacting with people.

If you are not able to focus on work / academics.

Feeling tired or lethargic.
